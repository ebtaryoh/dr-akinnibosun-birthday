The Journey

69 Years of
Faithfulness & Purpose

Every chapter written by the hand of God. Every season a testimony.

1957

Chosen Before Time

On March 17, 1957, God breathed life into a destined vessel. A child born with purpose etched into his very soul — Dr C.I Akinnibosun arrived, and heaven rejoiced.

1970s

Roots of Knowledge

Cultivating a sharp mind and tender heart. Years of learning, formation, and seeking — the early foundations of a man who would one day teach thousands.

1980s

Called & Consecrated

The call of God became unmistakable. Consecrated for ministry, he stepped into the pulpit with the Word burning in his bones — and never looked back.

1990s

Pastor, Father, Shepherd

Leading a congregation with grace, raising a family with love, and shepherding souls through life's valleys. A decade of faithful service that bore eternal fruit.

2000s

A Teacher of the Word

The depth of his teaching became his hallmark. Unlocking scripture, discipling believers, and raising sons in the faith — his classroom extended beyond four walls.

2010s

Legacy Builder

Watching the fruit of decades of ministry blossom. Children grown, grandchildren arriving, churches edified. The harvest of a life well-sown in God's kingdom.

2020s

Still Burning, Still Giving

The fire never dimmed. Still teaching, still shepherding, still pouring out. A living proof that God's grace is sufficient and His calling is without repentance.

2026

69 & Glorious 🎉

The crown chapter of a life surrendered to God. Today the family, the church, and the kingdom declare: Well done, good and faithful servant! Happy 69th Birthday, Sir!
